# Pre-Benchmark QUBO Formulation Audit

This audit documents the active and inactive terms in the QUBO model generated by `qubo_builder.py` and solved by `qaoa_cudaq_solver.py` and `qubo_sa_solver.py`.

---

## Active Terms Summary

| Term | Active? | Coefficient Name | Default Value | Optimization (SA) | Optimization (QAOA) | Optimization (CP-SAT) |
| :--- | :---: | :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Assignment Uniqueness** | **YES** | `alpha_assign` | `10.0` | Optimized | Optimized | Equivalent hard constraint |
| **GPU Compatibility** | **YES** | `alpha_gpu_compat` | `0.0` (set to `10.0`) | Optimized | Optimized | Equivalent hard constraint |

| **Objective Term** | **YES** | `objective_scale` | `1.0` (set to `0.1`) | Optimized | Optimized | Equivalent objective |

## Detailed Active Terms

### 1. Assignment Uniqueness Penalty
* **Coefficient Name**: `alpha_assign`
* **Default Value**: `10.0`
* **Mathematical Form**: $\alpha_{\text{assign}} \sum_i (1 - \sum_j x_{ij})^2$
* **Entry in Q matrix**:
  * **Diagonal**: Subtracts $\alpha_{\text{assign}}$ from $Q[idx][idx]$ for all job-node variables.
  * **Off-diagonal**: Adds $2.0 \times \alpha_{\text{assign}}$ to $Q[idx_j][idx_k]$ for all pairs of nodes $(j, k)$ associated with the same job.
* **Optimization Support**:
  * **SA**: Yes, minimizes QUBO energy containing these matrix terms.
  * **QAOA**: Yes, mapped directly to Ising Z-Z and Z coupling terms.
  * **CP-SAT**: Handled as a hard constraint: `model.Add(sum(choices) == 1)` for each job.
* **Variable Count Impact**: None (no auxiliary/slack variables needed).

### 2. GPU Compatibility Penalty
* **Coefficient Name**: `alpha_gpu_compat`
* **Default Value**: `0.0` (in `qubo_builder.py` defaults; explicitly passed as `10.0` in the QAOA pipeline).
* **Mathematical Form**: $\alpha_{\text{gpu\_compat}} \sum_{i,j \mid \text{job } i \text{ needs GPU}, \text{ node } j \text{ is CPU}} x_{ij}$
* **Entry in Q matrix**: Adds $\alpha_{\text{gpu\_compat}}$ to diagonal $Q[idx][idx]$ for incompatible job-node pairs.
* **Optimization Support**:
  * **SA**: Yes.
  * **QAOA**: Yes, as a single-qubit Z-field shift.
  * **CP-SAT**: Excluded completely from decision variables (`if not compatible(job, node): continue`), which acts as an implicit hard constraint.
* **Variable Count Impact**: None.

### 3. Capacity Penalty
* **Coefficient Name**: `alpha_capacity`
* **Default Value**: `10.0`
* **Mathematical Form**: $\alpha_{\text{capacity}} \sum_j [(\sum_i \text{cpu}_i x_{ij} - \text{cpu\_cap}_j)^2 + (\sum_i \text{gpu}_i x_{ij} - \text{gpu\_cap}_j)^2]$
* **Entry in Q matrix**:
  * **Diagonal**: Adds $\alpha_{\text{capacity}} (\text{demand}_i^2 - 2 \cdot \text{capacity}_j \cdot \text{demand}_i)$ to $Q[idx_j][idx_j]$ for all jobs on node $j$.
  * **Off-diagonal**: Adds $2.0 \times \alpha_{\text{capacity}} \cdot \text{demand}_i \cdot \text{demand}_k$ to $Q[idx_{ij}][idx_{kj}]$ for all pairs of jobs on node $j$.
* **Optimization Support**:
  * **SA**: Yes.
  * **QAOA**: Yes.
  * **CP-SAT**: **No**. CP-SAT mapping baseline does not constrain aggregate capacity because scheduling delays are resolved in the deterministic schedule decoder.
* **Variable Count Impact**: None (formulated as a direct squared penalty without slack variables).

### 4. Objective Term (Runtime surrogate)
* **Coefficient Name**: `objective_scale`
* **Default Value**: `1.0` (passed as `0.1` in QAOA pipeline, `1.0` in SA pipeline).
* **Mathematical Form**: $\text{scale} \sum_{i,j} \text{cost}(i, j) x_{ij}$, where $\text{cost}(i, j) = \text{estimated\_runtime}_i / \text{node\_capacity}_j$.
* **Entry in Q matrix**: Adds $\text{scale} \cdot \text{cost}(i,j)$ to diagonal $Q[idx][idx]$.
* **Optimization Support**:
  * **SA**: Yes.
  * **QAOA**: Yes.
  * **CP-SAT**: Yes, CP-SAT minimizes the identical linear sum: `model.Minimize(sum(var * node_cost_proxy(job, node)))`.
* **Variable Count Impact**: None.
